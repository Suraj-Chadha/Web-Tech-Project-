NEW DELHI:A day after he was attacked with chilli powder by a man inside the Delhi Secretariat, Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal Wednesday said forces inimical to the AAP’s growth want hone. Chilli attack on Delhi CM: Accused’s FB profile tells a tale of contradictions

In his purported Facebook profile, Anil Kumar Sharma, who allegedly assaulted Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal Tuesday, identifies himself as a “BJP worker”. His timeline is also replete with posts praising the Congress, though of late, he has been critical of the party. In several posts this month, Sharma has also used abusive words against Kejriwal and his deputy Manish Sisodia.

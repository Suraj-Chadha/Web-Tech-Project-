Mumbai News:-

Shortage of Highway Police personnel cripples efforts to enforce rules on Pune-Mumbai Expressway

Last week, SHP officials held a meeting that was also attended by police personnel from Pune, Raigad and Navi Mumbai, officials from the Maharashtra State Road Development Corporation and IRB Infrastructure. The meeting was attended by office-bearers of SaveLIFE Foundation, which is working towards reducing fatalities on the Expressway, as well as independent researcher Tanmay Pendse.

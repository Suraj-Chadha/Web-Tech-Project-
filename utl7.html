France seek rare repeat in ‘last’ Davis Cup final against Croatia

This year’s final will be the last in its current guise after the International Tennis Federation’s decision to revamp the team event starting in 2019. It has not been a pain-free process and fans and players alike will lament the scrapping of the ‘home and away’ format which has been such an integral part of the Davis Cup’s charm.

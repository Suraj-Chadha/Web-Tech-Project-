Chain reaction caused fiery crash on Brooklyn Bridge: Police

The fiery 7 a.m. crash on the Brooklyn-bound side of the bridge sent a column of black smoke rising above the landmark span. Motorists blocked by the wreck got out their cars to watch a swarm of firefighters work to extinguish the blaze. Police identified the victim who died as Kristopher Hambas, 32, of Scarsdale, New York. Six people were injured, including two who were hospitalized with non-life threatening injuries. The others refused medical attention.

Malaysian First Lady had a special request for Imran Khan

Pakistani Prime Minister Imran Khan has a new fan. During his two-day visit to Malaysia, First Lady Siti Hasmah Mohamad Ali had a request for him. “Prime minister, can I hold your hand?” the nonagenarian asked at an official photo-op. “Sure,” the cricketer-turned-politician replied with a smile, prompting laughter from those in the audience, and from his counterpart Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad.

I fear about quitting cricket: Wasim Jaffer after crossing 11000-run mark in Ranji Trophy

Wasim Jaffer on Wednesday became the first batsman to reach the 11000-run mark in Ranji Trophy history. The veteran batsman, who shifted his base from Mumbai to Vidarbha few seasons ago, slammed 153 against Baroda at the VCA Stadium in Nagpur. He spoke to The Indian Express about his landmark milestone, and why he is afraid to quit cricket at this moment.

France seek rare repeat in ‘last’ Davis Cup final against Croatia

This year’s final will be the last in its current guise after the International Tennis Federation’s decision to revamp the team event starting in 2019.
